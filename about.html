Our Mission

Founded in 2024, EcoFresh started with a simple question: "Why should a clean home mean a dirty planet?" We believe that hygiene shouldn't come at the cost of the environment.

Our team of scientists and nature-lovers work together to create cleaning solutions that are as effective as traditional brands but as gentle as a spring breeze.
